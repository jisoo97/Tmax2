HOW TO PLAY
"BUlK" is a game which is form of scoring points by matching notes along with music.
Difficulty of the selected song is divided into 'EASY(4KEY), 'NORMAL(6KEY)', HARD(8KEY).
The verdict is 'PERFECT','GOOD','NORMAL','BAD' in order according to accuracy.
